Hello!

Hello! I am currently a Ph.D. student and NSF Graduate Research Fellow studying theoretical astro-particle physics in the UC Irvine Department of Physics and Astronomy. I started this website as way of documenting my progress as I work to improve upon my statistics / data science / coding skills. See my blog on this page if you're curious about some of the side projects I've been working on.

My research focuses on using astrophysical and cosmological observations to probe the particle nature of dark matter. If you're interested in my research work, check out this bit of my page that describes some of the stuff I've worked on as a graduate student.

Outside of my research activities, I also enjoy helping students of all ages get excited about physics and the Universe through teaching and outreach programs. I've put some of my education materials on the education and outreach section of this site.
